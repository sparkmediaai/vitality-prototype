Meet Allyse Hargrove

Licensed & Board-Certified Massage Therapist serving clients in Nevada and Georgia with expert care since 2016.

Expert Care & Technical Excellence

Allyse Hargrove is a highly skilled and intuitive Licensed and Board-Certified Massage Therapist, proudly serving clients in both Nevada and Georgia. Since 2016, she has been dedicated to delivering high-quality patient care, blending technical expertise with a deep understanding of the body's biomechanics.

With a keen eye for assessment, Allyse excels at identifying the root causes of muscular tension, dysfunction, and pain. She takes a personalized approach to every session—carefully evaluating posture, movement patterns, and areas of discomfort to determine the most effective treatment plan.

"Our goal is not only to alleviate discomfort but to empower clients with the tools and knowledge they need to maintain long-term wellness."

Her ability to adapt pressure, technique, and modality ensures that each client receives targeted, results-driven therapy tailored to their specific needs.

Specialized Techniques & Modalities

Allyse specializes in Neuromuscular Therapy, Myofascial Release, and Sports Massage, using these techniques and others to address chronic and acute injuries, correct postural imbalances, and enhance overall human performance.
